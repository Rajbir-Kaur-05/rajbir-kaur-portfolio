About Me

Hello, I am Rajbir Kaur

RK

Hello! My name is Rajbir Kaur. I was born and raised in Amritsar, Punjab, India, and moved to Canada with my family in 2019. I currently live and work in Surrey, British Columbia, on the traditional, ancestral, and unceded lands of the Coast Salish peoples.

I hold a Bachelor of Science, a Bachelor of Education, and a Master of Mathematics. Before moving to Canada, I taught mathematics for ten years. I now work as a Teaching Assistant at an independent elementary school in Surrey, where I enjoy supporting students in their learning, building their confidence, and helping them reach their full potential.

I am passionate about creating a caring, inclusive, and respectful learning environment where every student feels valued, supported, and encouraged to succeed. I believe that positive relationships and a love of lifelong learning help students grow both academically and personally.
